Your Digital Shield: How to Stay Safe

Protecting yourself doesn't have to be complicated. By adopting a few key habits, you can build a strong defense against most common cyber threats. Explore the tips below to learn how to strengthen your security. Hover over each card for more details.

🔐

Use Strong Passwords

Password Best Practices

Create unique and complex passphrases for each account. A password manager can help you generate and store them securely, so you only have to remember one master password.

📲

Enable 2FA/MFA

Two-Factor Authentication

This adds a second layer of security. Even if someone steals your password, they won't be able to log in without a second code, usually sent to your phone. Enable it wherever possible.

🔄

Keep Software Updated

Patch Your Systems

Software updates often contain critical security patches that fix vulnerabilities. Regularly update your operating system, web browser, and other applications to stay protected.

📧

Think Before You Click

Be Skeptical of Links

Be cautious of unsolicited emails, messages, or attachments. If an offer seems too good to be true, it probably is. Verify the sender before clicking any links or downloading files.

🌐

Use Secure Networks

Beware of Public Wi-Fi

Avoid accessing sensitive information like bank accounts on public Wi-Fi networks. If you must use one, consider a VPN (Virtual Private Network) to encrypt your connection.

💾

Back Up Your Data

Protect Your Files

Regularly back up your important files to an external hard drive or a secure cloud service. This protects you from data loss due to hardware failure, theft, or a ransomware attack.

🗣️

Spot Social Engineering

Recognize Manipulation

Attackers often use psychology to trick you. They create a sense of urgency, fear, or curiosity to rush you into making a mistake. Always take a moment to think before you act.

🛡️

Use Antivirus

Install Security Software

A good antivirus program is essential. It runs in the background, scanning for and blocking malware before it can cause damage. Keep it enabled and updated.
